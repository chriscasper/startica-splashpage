Startica is a customizable homepage designed to help you protect and empower your loved ones online — especially parents, grandparents, and family members who could use a little digital guidance.

With Startica, you can create a safe, simple, and welcoming web experience by curating the most important links they'll use every day — from trusted banking sites and shopping portals to news, weather, and even hobbies like select subreddits or recipe blogs.
